About Me

My first encounter with coding was a Python-based 'Intro to Software Engineering' course at university. My 17 year old self was unconvinced by first year engineering, and decided to set aside my natural aptitude for the Maths and Sciences to pursue a humanities path instead.

I spent the next 10 years working as a languages teacher in Australia and Japan, but after discovering "Codecademy", ended up teaching myself HTML, CSS and basic Javascript as a hobby. Eventually, I decided to use my love of learning languages to learn how to code properly.

Now, I have completed a 12-week full-stack bootcamp, where I worked on various full-stack development projects. The projects I have created can be viewed above. To me, software development is the perfect mix of logic, creativity, and that really satisfying feeling you get when you solve a difficult problem.
